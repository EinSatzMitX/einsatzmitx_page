Hollow Knight

Hollow Knight and it's successor Hollow Knight: Silksong are two of the biggest Indie games out there. From the Story, to gameplay mechanics to artwork and especially music. The game has it all. The fan base of the game is absolutely gigantic. Hollow Knight is the game I have the most playtime in from my steam library and I would do anything to experience the game like I did the first time I played it.
